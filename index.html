Operating Principles

Three commitments that shape every line of code.

i.

Sovereign by design

Customer data, code, and credentials stay within the customer's perimeter. We build self-hosted by default and pull only what is strictly required. Law over code, in that order.

ii.

Built for institutions

Our buyers are ministries, hospitals, banks, and enterprises — anywhere on earth. We tag every capability claim as verified, probable, inferred, or speculative. Honest disclosure is commercially stronger with serious buyers.

iii.

Alexandria origin. Global reach.

The Company

Alexandria is the origin. The world is the market.

Elmahrosa International was founded in 2007 in Alexandria as an events and production company. In 2020, the company pivoted to software and AI development — launching its first technical products and establishing the engineering foundation that became the TEOS stack.

The thesis is simple: the next generation of sovereign AI infrastructure would not be built only in San Francisco or London — it would be built wherever the engineering is serious and the institutional need is real. Alexandria qualifies on both counts.

We build for governments, hospitals, ministries, and banks. Today the TEOS stack spans 81 repositories, 4 live products, and institutional buyers across multiple regions.
